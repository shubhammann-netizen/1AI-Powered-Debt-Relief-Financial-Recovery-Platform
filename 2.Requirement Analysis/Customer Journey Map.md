# Customer Journey Map
*Borrower journey through the FinRelief AI platform*

---

## Stage 1 — Discovery & Registration

The borrower creates an account (name, email, password) and logs in. A JWT access token is issued and stored client-side for all subsequent requests.

## Stage 2 — Profile & Loan Setup

The borrower enters their monthly income, monthly expenses, and existing debts (Financial_Profiles), then adds each loan they hold (Loans): type, original amount, outstanding balance, interest rate, and due date.

## Stage 3 — Financial Health Review

The Dashboard displays computed EMI-to-income ratio, debt-to-income ratio, monthly surplus, total outstanding debt, and an overall financial health score with a Low/Medium/High stress badge.

## Stage 4 — Settlement Prediction

On the Settlement Predictor page, the borrower selects a loan and requests a prediction; the system returns a recommended settlement amount, a likelihood category, and a priority level, stored in Settlement_Records.

## Stage 5 — AI Negotiation

On the Negotiation Email page, the borrower selects a loan and (optionally) a lender name; the AI Negotiation Engine returns a strategy summary, a full settlement letter, and a plain-language explanation, stored in AI_History.

## Stage 6 — Review & Follow-Up

The History page lets the borrower revisit every past AI-generated strategy and letter, and the Know Your Rights page gives general borrower-rights guidance before they contact a lender.
