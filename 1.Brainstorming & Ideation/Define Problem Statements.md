*FinRelief AI — AI Powered Debt Relief & Financial Recovery Platform*

# Define Problem Statements
*Framing the core problems FinRelief AI addresses*

---

## Problem Statement 1

Borrowers with multiple active loans (credit cards, personal loans, auto loans, etc.) often cannot tell which debt is most urgent to address first, leading to missed payments on high-priority accounts while lower-priority balances are paid down.

## Problem Statement 2

Borrowers rarely know what settlement percentage a lender is likely to accept, so they either offer too little (rejected outright) or too much (overpaying relative to what could have been negotiated).

## Problem Statement 3

Writing a professional, persuasive settlement negotiation letter requires financial and legal literacy that many borrowers in financial distress do not have time or expertise to develop.

## Problem Statement 4

Existing personal-finance tools track spending but do not connect financial health directly to an actionable settlement or negotiation plan.

## Target Users

- Individual borrowers managing 2 or more active loans/credit lines
- Borrowers who have missed one or more repayment deadlines
- Users who want a data-driven, private alternative to hiring a debt-settlement agency
