*FinRelief AI — AI Powered Debt Relief & Financial Recovery Platform*

# Brainstorming & Idea Prioritization
*Ideation session output for the FinRelief AI concept*

---

## Problem Context

Millions of borrowers fall behind on multiple loans and credit obligations without a clear, personalized view of which debts to prioritize, what a realistic settlement looks like, or how to negotiate with a lender. Manual debt counseling is expensive, slow, and not available to everyone.

## Ideas Generated

- A chatbot that only answers general debt questions (rejected — no personalization, no action taken on behalf of the user).
- A pure spreadsheet-based EMI/DTI calculator (rejected — useful but no negotiation support or AI guidance).
- A marketplace connecting borrowers to human debt-settlement agencies (rejected — slow, costly, and raises trust concerns).
- An AI-powered platform that analyzes the borrower's financial profile, predicts settlement amounts per loan, and auto-generates negotiation letters (selected).
- A rules-only settlement calculator with no AI negotiation writing (kept as the fallback layer for reliability when AI is unavailable).

## Prioritization Method

Ideas were scored on three axes — borrower impact, technical feasibility within the project timeline, and differentiation from existing tools (spreadsheets, generic chatbots, human debt counselors). The AI-powered settlement and negotiation platform scored highest on all three axes and was carried forward as the FinRelief AI concept.

## Selected Direction

FinRelief AI: a web platform that stores a borrower's loans and financial profile, computes financial health metrics, predicts settlement percentages per loan, and uses Google Gemini (with a rule-based fallback) to draft negotiation strategies and lender-ready settlement letters.
