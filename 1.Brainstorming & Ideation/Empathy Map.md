# Empathy Map
*Understanding the FinRelief AI borrower persona*

---

## Persona

"Overwhelmed Borrower" — a working adult juggling 2-4 loans/credit obligations, at least one of which is overdue.

## Says

- "I don't know which loan to pay off first."
- "I'm scared to call my lender because I don't know what to offer."
- "I can't afford a financial advisor right now."

## Thinks

- "Is my credit already ruined?"
- "What if I offer too little and they refuse?"
- "I wish someone could just tell me the numbers."

## Does

- Searches the web for generic debt advice
- Avoids lender calls out of anxiety
- Makes partial or inconsistent payments across accounts

## Feels

- Anxious about financial stability
- Embarrassed to ask for help
- Hopeful when given a clear, concrete plan

## Pain Points

- No single view of total debt and urgency
- No idea what settlement amount is realistic
- No confidence writing to a lender

## Gains FinRelief AI Provides

- A prioritized view of all loans
- A data-driven settlement recommendation per loan
- A ready-to-send negotiation letter and a plain-language explanation of the strategy
