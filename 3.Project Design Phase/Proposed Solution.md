*FinRelief AI — AI Powered Debt Relief & Financial Recovery Platform*

# Proposed Solution
*High-level solution overview*

---

## Overview

FinRelief AI is a secure, full-stack web application in which a borrower registers, records their loans and financial profile, and receives automatically computed financial health metrics, settlement predictions, and AI-generated negotiation content for each loan.

## Key Modules

- Authentication & Session Management — JWT-based login with hashed passwords.
- Financial Engine — EMI/DTI ratio calculation, stress classification, loan priority.
- Settlement Prediction System — settlement percentage and recommended amount per loan.
- AI Negotiation Strategy Engine — Gemini-generated (or rule-based) strategy, letter, and summary.
- History & Reporting — persisted record of every AI negotiation package generated.

## Value Proposition

Borrowers get an end-to-end, self-serve debt-recovery workflow — from understanding their financial position to sending a data-backed settlement offer — without needing to hire a paid debt-settlement agency.

## Differentiator

Unlike static calculators or generic chatbots, FinRelief AI ties financial analysis directly to a generated, ready-to-send negotiation letter, and remains functional even without live AI access via its rule-based fallback engine.
