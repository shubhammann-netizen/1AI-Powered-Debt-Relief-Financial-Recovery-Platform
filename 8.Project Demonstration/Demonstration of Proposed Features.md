*FinRelief AI — AI Powered Debt Relief & Financial Recovery Platform*

# Demonstration of Proposed Features
*Live-demo script covering every core feature*

---

## Demo Flow

- 1. Register a new borrower account and log in.
- 2. Add 2-3 loans of different types (e.g. Credit Card, Personal Loan, Auto Loan).
- 3. Fill in the financial profile (income, expenses, existing debts) and show the Dashboard's EMI ratio, DTI ratio, surplus, and health score update.
- 4. Open the Settlement Predictor, generate a prediction for the most overdue loan, and show the recommended settlement amount and priority badge.
- 5. Open the Negotiation Email page, generate an AI negotiation package, and read through the strategy, letter, and plain-language summary.
- 6. Visit the History page to show the generated package was persisted.
- 7. Visit Know Your Rights to show the supporting borrower-guidance content.

## Fallback Demonstration

To demonstrate resilience, the demo can be run with no GEMINI_API_KEY configured — the negotiation letter is still generated instantly via the rule-based fallback engine, proving the platform degrades gracefully.
